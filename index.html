Your security cameras, on the big screen.

CamWall turns your Apple TV into a live security-camera wall. See every camera at once in a beautiful grid, or any one full-screen — with instant reconnection when a stream drops.

Works with virtually any camera

  • Universal RTSP — any IP camera or NVR that serves an RTSP stream
  • ONVIF auto-discovery — scan your network, pick your camera, and CamWall fills in the stream URLs for you
  • Smart quality — low-res sub-streams in the grid, full resolution in fullscreen
  • Self-healing wall — automatic reconnection with clear status on every tile
