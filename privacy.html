Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

CamWall does not collect, store, or share any personal data. No analytics, no tracking, no advertising, no account.

Your cameras stay yours

CamWall connects directly from your Apple TV to the cameras and NVRs you configure on your local network. Video streams are played on-device and are never sent to us or any third party. CamWall has no server.

Credentials

Camera usernames and passwords are stored securely in the Apple Keychain on your Apple TV and are used only to connect to your cameras.

Purchases

CamWall is a one-time paid purchase handled entirely by Apple; we never receive your payment details.
