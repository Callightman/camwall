FAQ

How do I find my camera's RTSP URL? Try Discover first (Add Camera → Discover → Scan): if your camera supports ONVIF, CamWall fills the URLs automatically. Otherwise check your camera or NVR's manual — most list an RTSP path like rtsp://IP:554/stream1.

The tile says Reconnecting… CamWall retries automatically every few seconds. Check that the camera is powered and on the same network as your Apple TV.

How do I edit or remove a camera? Press and hold the camera's tile, then choose Edit or Remove.

Does CamWall record? No — CamWall is a live viewer. Recording stays on your NVR/camera.
